EMR Project — OpenMRS / KenyaEMR

We're building an Electronic Medical Record (EMR) using OpenMRS and the KenyaEMR distribution as our base. The goal is to deliver a production-ready EMR that integrates with Kenyan Ministry of Health reporting (DHIS2), supports clinical workflows, and integrates with local services (M-Pesa, SMS).

Planned phases

  1. Requirements & MoH report inventory (OPD, ANC, Immunization)
  2. Dev environment: Docker OpenMRS + KenyaEMR, seed CIEL concepts
  3. Prototype one report and DHIS2 integration
  4. Pilot at facility, iterate, then production rollout

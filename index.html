About Mwein Medical Services

We are a licensed private community medical clinic providing comprehensive healthcare to individuals and families in Mungatsi and surrounding areas. Our facility operates round-the-clock with qualified clinical staff and modern diagnostic equipment, ensuring reliable care when you need it.

Our Core Services

Comprehensive healthcare services available 24/7

Outpatient Services (OPD)

Medical consultations, diagnosis, and treatment for acute and chronic conditions. Available for walk-ins and pre-booked appointments.

Laboratory Services

Diagnostic testing including malaria screening, blood tests, infectious disease screening, and urine/stool analysis.

Ultrasound Imaging

Safe, non-invasive ultrasound for pregnancy monitoring, abdominal evaluation, and other diagnostic imaging needs.

Pharmacy Services

Professional dispensing of prescribed medications with guidance on usage, dosage, and treatment adherence.

Antenatal Care (ANC)

Comprehensive pregnancy care including routine check-ups, laboratory screening, ultrasound imaging, and maternal health education.

Chronic Disease Management

Long-term care for hypertension, diabetes, sickle cell disease, and other chronic conditions requiring ongoing support.

Understanding Diabetes

Educational content only. Clinical management should be under a healthcare provider.

Types of diabetes

Type 1 diabetes is usually autoimmune and requires insulin. Type 2 diabetes is related to insulin resistance and lifestyle factors but may also need medication or insulin.

Signs and monitoring

Common signs include increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, and unexplained weight changes. Regular blood sugar monitoring and periodic laboratory tests help guide treatment.

Management principles

Management includes healthy diet, physical activity, medication as prescribed, and regular clinical follow-up to prevent complications.

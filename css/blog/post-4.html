Antenatal Care: Healthy Pregnancy

Educational content only. Follow personalized advice from your healthcare provider.

What antenatal care includes

Routine antenatal care (ANC) includes regular clinical checks, blood and urine tests, ultrasound scans where indicated, immunizations, nutritional advice, and health education to support safe pregnancy and delivery.

When to start ANC

Begin ANC as early as possible in pregnancy, ideally in the first trimester. Early care improves outcomes for mother and baby.

Key topics covered during visits

  • Assessment of maternal and fetal health
  • Screening tests and immunizations
  • Nutrition and lifestyle advice
  • Birth planning and danger-sign recognition

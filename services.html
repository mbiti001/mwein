Scope of Services

Mwein Medical Services is a private community healthcare facility located in Mungatsi, Busia County. We provide comprehensive outpatient medical care supported by diagnostic services, pharmacy services, and continuity of care for chronic conditions. The facility operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and accepts SHA (Social Health Authority) for eligible services.

SafeCare Compliance: We operate in accordance with SafeCare quality standards and Kenya's healthcare regulations. Our services are delivered by qualified healthcare professionals using evidence-based clinical practices.

Clinical & Diagnostic Services

Complete healthcare services for individuals and families

Outpatient Services (OPD)

Medical consultations, diagnosis, and treatment of acute and chronic conditions for adults and children. Available for walk-ins and pre-booked appointments.

Laboratory Services

Routine diagnostic testing including malaria screening, blood tests, infectious disease screening, and urine/stool analysis to support clinical decision-making.

Virtual Consultation

Remote telemedicine services for non-emergency medical advice, follow-up care, prescription refills, and chronic disease management through secure communication channels.

Ultrasound Imaging

Safe and non-invasive ultrasound for pregnancy monitoring, abdominal evaluation, pelvic examination, and other diagnostic imaging using modern equipment.

Pharmacy Services

Professional dispensing of prescribed medications with guidance on usage, dosage, and adherence to treatment regimens.

Antenatal Care (ANC)

Comprehensive pregnancy care including routine check-ups, laboratory screening, ultrasound imaging, and maternal health education.

Chronic Disease Management

Long-term management of chronic conditions including hypertension, diabetes, sickle cell disease, and other conditions requiring ongoing care.

Comprehensive Family Planning Services

We provide a full range of family planning methods to support reproductive health choices. Our trained healthcare professionals offer counseling, insertion, management, and follow-up care for all modern contraceptive methods.

Intrauterine Devices (IUDs)

Copper IUDs (Paragard) and Hormonal IUDs (Mirena, Skyla) - Highly effective, long-acting reversible contraception. Insertion, monitoring, and removal services available.

Implants

Subdermal contraceptive implants (Implanon, Nexplanon) - Small rod inserted under arm skin for 3-5 years protection. Insertion and removal services provided.

Oral Contraceptives

Birth Control Pills (combined and progestin-only) - Prescription, counseling on usage, and management of side effects.

Injectable Contraceptives

Depo-Provera (DMPA) and other injected methods - Quarterly or monthly injections for effective pregnancy prevention with counseling and follow-up.

Barrier Methods

Male condoms, female condoms, and diaphragms - Guidance on correct usage, availability, and STI prevention counseling.

Fertility Awareness Methods

Lactational Amenorrhea Method (LAM) and cycle-tracking methods - Education on natural family planning approaches with effectiveness counseling.

Bilateral Tubal Ligation (BTL)

Permanent sterilization referral: We provide comprehensive pre-procedure counseling, post-operative care, and complications management. For BTL procedures, we connect you with our trusted referral centers for safe, professional surgical services.

About Bilateral Tubal Ligation (BTL) Referrals

BTL is a permanent surgical sterilization procedure. While we do not perform BTL at our facility, we provide comprehensive support:

  • Detailed counseling about the procedure, benefits, and permanent nature
  • Referrals to accredited surgical centers with experienced specialists
  • Pre-operative evaluation and medical clearance
  • Post-operative care and complication management

Laboratory Services & Tests

Comprehensive diagnostic testing conducted by qualified laboratory technicians

Malaria Testing

Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) for quick screening and blood film microscopy for confirmation and parasite counting.

Complete Blood Tests

Full Blood Count (FBC), hemoglobin measurement, blood sugar testing, and blood grouping with blood typing.

Infectious Disease Screening

HIV rapid testing with post-test counseling, VDRL (syphilis screening), HBsAg (hepatitis B), and H. pylori testing.

Urine & Stool Analysis

Urinalysis (dipstick) and microscopy, stool microscopy for parasite screening and detection.

Pregnancy Testing

Urine pregnancy test (beta-hCG) for rapid detection of pregnancy.

Pathology & Screening

Blood films for microscopy analysis, specialized testing, and laboratory-based diagnostics to support clinical care.

Important: All laboratory test results are reviewed, interpreted, and explained by qualified clinical staff in the context of patient history, clinical examination, and relevant findings. Test results alone do not constitute a diagnosis. Patients should discuss their results with their healthcare provider.

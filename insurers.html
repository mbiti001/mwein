About Our Facility

Mwein Medical Services is a private community healthcare facility located in Mungatsi, Busia County, Kenya. We are dedicated to providing accessible, professional, and compassionate healthcare services to our community.

Our facility is equipped to provide outpatient medical care, diagnostic laboratory services, ultrasound imaging, pharmacy services, antenatal care, immunization services, and management of chronic diseases. We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to ensure healthcare access whenever patients need it.

Who Qualifies for Bill Waiver

Patients may qualify for full or partial medical bill waiver if they meet one or more of:

  • Monthly household income below Ksh 5,000
  • Unemployed or unable to work due to medical condition
  • Orphan (below 18) or widow/widower with dependents
  • Person with severe disability (100% impairment)
  • Emergency/urgent care preventing financial hardship
  • Recommended by community health worker or local elder
  • Referred by another healthcare provider due to poverty

Application Process:

Speak with our clinic staff at time of visit. No special documentation required for most cases. Our team will assess need and approve waivers based on established criteria.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Mwein Medical Services is a registered healthcare facility operating under Kenya's healthcare regulations. Donations qualify for tax deduction. We'll provide official receipts upon request that you can use for your tax returns.

Can I donate a different amount?

Absolutely! Select "Custom Amount" and enter any amount you wish. Every shilling helps. You can also call us at +254 707 711 888 or WhatsApp for alternative payment methods if needed.

Can I set up a monthly donation?

Yes! Monthly donors help us plan facility improvements more effectively. Contact us via WhatsApp or phone at +254 707 711 888 to set up a recurring monthly donation. We'll handle the logistics with you.

How will I know my donation was received?

You'll receive M-Pesa confirmation immediately. Please share your screenshot with us via WhatsApp so we can send you an official donation receipt. You'll also receive periodic updates on how your donation is making a difference.

Who decides if a patient qualifies for bill waiver?

Our clinic management team reviews each case. Patients can speak with our staff during their visit about financial constraints. We have clear criteria (poverty level, unemployment, disability, emergency cases) that are consistently applied. The decision is made within 24-48 hours.

Can I ask for a quarterly report on donations?

Yes! Donors can request quarterly financial reports on how their donations are used. Email mweinmedical@gmail.com with your request, or visit our facility. We maintain detailed records of all donations and their allocation.

Can I donate on behalf of someone else?

Yes! You can make donations as gifts, memorials, or in honor of someone. Please mention this when confirming via WhatsApp, and we'll recognize the contribution appropriately and provide special receipts if needed.
